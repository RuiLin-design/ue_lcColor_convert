Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure tool environment.

1. Information Collection and Processing

UE5 Color Converter is a strict client-side web application. We believe that your workflow data belongs to you. Therefore, we do not collect, transmit, intercept, or store any personal information, input values, HEX codes, or generated color parameters on our servers.

All color conversion mathematics, inverse gamma corrections, and state management are executed instantaneously within your local browser environment using JavaScript. You do not need to create an account or provide an email address to use our core services.

2. Local Storage (Color History)

To significantly improve your development workflow and user experience, this website utilizes your browser's native LocalStorage API. This is used exclusively to save your recent "Color History" so you don't lose your work if you accidentally refresh the page.

This data snippet is stored securely on your own device's hard drive and is never uploaded to any cloud service. You maintain full control over this data and can permanently delete it at any time by clicking the "Clear" button in the tool's interface, or by clearing your browser's site data/cache.

3. Third-Party Services (Analytics & Ads)

While the core tool is completely local, we do rely on industry-standard third-party services—specifically Google Analytics and Google AdSense—to monitor overall website traffic, understand user demographics (e.g., country of origin, browser type), and display relevant advertisements to help keep this tool free for the game development community.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites across the internet.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your customized browsing profile.
  • You maintain the right to opt out of personalized advertising. You can do so by visiting Google's Ad Settings.
  • For more information on how Google uses data when you use partners' sites, please visit Google's Policy Page.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or to comply with legal requirements. Any modifications will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices.
